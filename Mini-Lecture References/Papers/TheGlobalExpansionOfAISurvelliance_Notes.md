https://carnegieendowment.org/research/2019/09/the-global-expansion-of-ai-surveillance

This journal paper argues that AI enabled survaliance systems are becoming mainstream in the modern global world. 
	- Examples include: Liberal Democracies, Autatarian governments, and failed states
	- The paper found that China and the USA are the leading exporters of AI enabled survaliance 
	- The survaliance ranges from traffic cameras, conjestion mapping, to monitoring people in public spaces
At time of writting 75 of 176 countries imploy some form of AI assisted survaliance. 
	- Smart city/ Safe city platforms
	- Facial recognition systems
	- Smart policing 
China tends to be a major pusher of this technology through their "Belt and Road" initiative. 
	- 100% of the Belt and Road initiative recieving countries have accepted some form of Chinese AI survaliance technology
	- Huawei, Hikvision, Dahu, adn ZTE are the leading Chinese firms in AI survaliance
	- IBM, Palantir, and Cisco are the leading USA companies in AI survaliance 
Depolyment stats	
	- 51% of advanced democracies depoy AI surveillance systems
	- Only 37% of closed autocracties deploy AI surveillance. 
	- 41% of electoral democracies/illiberal democracies use AI surveillance technology
Despite the democracies representing the majority of AI surveillance use, autocratic and semi-autocratic countries are the most likely to abuse AI survelliance technology
